# Golden Egg Fund – Anti-Money Laundering (AML) / Know Your Customer (KYC) Policy
**Last updated:** 12-23-25

This Anti-Money Laundering (“AML”) and Know Your Customer (“KYC”) Policy outlines Golden Egg Fund’s approach to preventing money laundering, terrorist financing, and other illicit financial activity. This document is provided for compliance and partner review purposes.

## 1. Purpose
Golden Egg Fund is committed to complying with applicable anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing laws and regulations. This policy describes how Golden Egg Fund mitigates financial crime risk through reliance on regulated third-party service providers, subject to approval and availability.

## 2. Business Model Overview
Golden Egg Fund operates as a technology platform that enables users to access financial services through regulated third-party providers.

- Golden Egg Fund does not custody customer funds or securities  
- Golden Egg Fund does not execute trades directly  
- Brokerage, clearing, custody, and settlement services, when enabled, are intended to be provided by regulated broker-dealers, subject to partner approval  
- Bank account verification is intended to be performed through regulated third-party providers, where applicable  

## 3. AML & KYC Responsibility
Golden Egg Fund intends to rely on its regulated broker-dealer and financial service partners to perform AML and KYC obligations, including:
- Customer identification and verification (KYC)
- Sanctions and watchlist screening
- Anti-money laundering monitoring
- Suspicious activity detection and reporting (SARs)

Golden Egg Fund does not collect or store sensitive identity documents such as Social Security numbers or government-issued identification.

## 4. Transaction Monitoring
Transaction monitoring, including the detection of suspicious or unusual activity, is expected to be performed by regulated partners in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Golden Egg Fund may assist by:
- Cooperating with partner investigations
- Providing relevant platform usage information when legally requested

## 5. Reporting & Cooperation
Golden Egg Fund cooperates fully with lawful requests from regulators, financial institutions, and law enforcement agencies as required by applicable law.

## 6. Employee Responsibilities
Access to internal systems and data is restricted based on role and necessity. Golden Egg Fund personnel are expected to adhere to internal security, confidentiality, and data-handling standards.

## 7. Policy Review
This AML/KYC Policy is reviewed periodically and updated as necessary to reflect changes in business operations, regulatory requirements, or partner obligations.

---

Golden Egg Fund is not a registered broker-dealer or investment adviser.
