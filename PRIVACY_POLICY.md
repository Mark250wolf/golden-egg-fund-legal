# Golden Egg Fund – Privacy Policy
**Last updated:** 12-23-25

Golden Egg Fund (“Golden Egg Fund,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded when you use the Golden Egg Fund mobile application and related services (the “App”).

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:
- Name
- Email address
- Contact information
- Account credentials
- Communications you send to us (such as support requests or feedback)

### b. Financial Information via Third-Party Providers
When you choose to link a bank account, Golden Egg Fund may rely on regulated third-party service providers to securely access limited financial information, which may include:
- Bank name
- Account type
- Account balance
- Account ownership details

Golden Egg Fund does not receive or store your bank login credentials.

### c. Brokerage-Related Information
If brokerage features are enabled, brokerage account creation, funding, and investment transactions are intended to be handled by regulated third-party broker-dealers, subject to approval and availability. Golden Egg Fund does not custody customer funds or securities.

### d. Automatically Collected Information
We may collect limited technical and usage information, including:
- Device type
- Operating system
- App usage data
- IP address (for security, fraud prevention, and compliance purposes)

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App
- Facilitate account linking and verification through third-party providers
- Enable access to third-party brokerage services, where available
- Improve App functionality and user experience
- Communicate with you regarding updates or support
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

## 3. How We Share Information
We may share information with:
- Regulated third-party service providers that support App functionality (such as bank-linking or brokerage providers)
- Service providers that assist with hosting, analytics, or security
- Regulatory, legal, or governmental authorities when required by law

Golden Egg Fund does not sell your personal information.

## 4. Data Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. Sensitive data is transmitted using encrypted connections, and data is stored using reputable cloud service providers with industry-standard security measures. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the App and to comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements. You may request deletion of your account, subject to applicable laws and obligations.

## 6. Your Choices and Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent where applicable

You may exercise these rights by contacting us using the information below.
