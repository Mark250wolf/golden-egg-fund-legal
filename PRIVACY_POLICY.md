Golden Egg Fund – Privacy Policy  
Last updated: 12-23-25

Golden Egg Fund (“Golden Egg Fund,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Golden Egg Fund mobile application and related services (the “App”).

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:
- Name
- Email address
- Account credentials
- Contact information
- Communications you send to us (support requests, feedback)

### b. Financial Information via Third Parties
When you link a bank account, we use third-party providers (such as Plaid) to securely access limited financial information, which may include:
- Bank name
- Account type
- Account balance
- Account ownership details

Golden Egg Fund does not receive or store your bank login credentials.

### c. Brokerage Information
When trading is enabled, brokerage account creation, funding, and trading activities are handled by a regulated third-party broker. Brokerage services are provided by Alpaca Securities LLC, a registered broker-dealer and member FINRA/SIPC. Golden Egg Fund does not custody funds or securities.

### d. Automatically Collected Information
We may collect limited technical information, such as:
- Device type
- Operating system
- App usage data
- IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use information we collect to:
- Provide and operate the App
- Facilitate bank linking and account verification
- Enable brokerage account setup and trading through third-party providers
- Improve App functionality and user experience
- Communicate with you about updates or support
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

## 3. How We Share Information
We may share your information with:
- Third-party service providers (e.g., Plaid for bank linking, Alpaca Securities LLC for brokerage services)
- Regulatory or legal authorities when required by law
- Service providers who assist with hosting, analytics, or security

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Data Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information. Sensitive data is transmitted using encrypted connections. Data is stored using reputable cloud service providers and protected using industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest where applicable. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion of your account, subject to applicable laws and regulatory obligations.

## 6. Your Choices and Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent where applicable

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at the email below.
