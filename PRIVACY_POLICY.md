We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:
•	Name
•	Email address
•	Account credentials
•	Contact information
•	Communications you send to us (support requests, feedback)

When you link a bank account, we use third-party providers (such as Plaid) to securely access limited financial information, which may include:

When trading is enabled, brokerage account creation, funding, and trading activities are handled by a regulated third-party broker. Golden Egg Fund does not custody funds or securities.

We may collect limited technical information, such as:
•	Device type

•	App usage data
•	IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
